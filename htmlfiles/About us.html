This is a simple E-commerce website made for Web Development course in KLS Gogte Institute of Technology. The objective of this project is to minimize the difficulties of people by providing them daily needs simply. At Ezkart people can simply find their desired item and order it. You are provided both choices of "cash on delivery" as well as "Online payment" which you can choose according to your simplicity. We've created an initial prototype of the website.
